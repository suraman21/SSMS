About Our School

ስለ ትምህርት ቤታችን

Our Mission

To nurture young souls in the teachings of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahdo Church, fostering spiritual growth, moral values, and a deep connection with our faith and heritage.

የኛ ተልእኮ ልጆችን በኢትዮጵያ ኦርቶዶክስ ተዋሕዶ ቤተ ክርስቲያን ትምህርቶች እያሳደግን መንፈሳዊ እድገትን፣ የሞራል እሴቶችን እና ከእምነታችን እና ከቅርሶቻችን ጋር ጥልቅ ግንኙነት መፍጠር ነው።

Biblical Education

Comprehensive study of Holy Scripture in both Ge'ez and Amharic languages.

Cultural Heritage

Preservation and teaching of Ethiopian Orthodox traditions and practices.

Community Building

Creating a loving community where children grow together in faith.
